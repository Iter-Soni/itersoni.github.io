What is Iter Soni?

Iter Soni is a multifaceted creative professional specializing in Visual Effects design, VJing, Music Production, Composition, DJing, and Guitar performance. With a diverse skill set spanning across various artistic realms, Iter brings a unique blend of talents to every project. Currently residing in Argentina, Iter operates globally and is ready to take on any creative challenge.

Music

Iter Soni embodies a vision that views music as a form of unrestricted art, unconstrained by the limitations of genre labels. The primary goal of this project is to transform sound into an immersive journey for the listener, evoking the sensation of being transported to alternate realms through music. Iter Soni strives to create an experience where listeners feel as though they are in a different place entirely when engaging with the music. This intention to transport the audience to new dimensions lies at the core of Iter Soni's artistic pursuit.

What music genre is Iter Soni?

Iter Soni defies traditional genre categorization, aiming to create a unique and immersive auditory experience that transcends the boundaries of specific music genres. While it draws inspiration from various genres, such as Progressive House, Techno, Hypnotic Techno, and Minimal, Iter Soni seamlessly blends these influences to craft its distinctive sound. The project incorporates elements from Progressive Rock and Metal, utilizing distorted electric guitars and drums to infuse the music with intensity and energy. Additionally, Iter Soni's compositions are structured with influences from cinematic music, employing techniques such as leitmotifs and melodies to evoke different emotions and situations within the sound.In summary, Iter Soni's music is a fusion of diverse influences and genres, unified by its overarching vision to transport listeners on a sonic journey beyond the confines of traditional genre labels.
